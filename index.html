AKAN NAMES CALCULATOR APP

"Enter your birthday and the app will tell you the day of the week were you born."

About The App

This web application will take user's birthday and calculate the day of the week they were born and then depending on their gender outputs their Akan Name. The application should check whether the date and month entered is valid. if either of them is invalid, the user should be alerted and asked to enter a valid one.

Akan Names.(Meaning)

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian day names.

Weekdays and names

MALE:
Sunday: Kwasi, Monday: Kwadwo, Tuesday: Kwabena, Wednesday: Kwaku, Thursday:  Yaw, Friday: Kofi, Saturday: Kwame
FEMALE:
Sunday: Akosua, Monday: Adwoa, Tuesday: Abenaa, Wednesday: Akua, Thursday:  Yaa, Friday: Afua, Saturday: Ama
